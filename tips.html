Studying methods: Finding your way of learning can make your Learning journey easier

Visual learning

They are keen on puzzles and graphs. They mostly learn by the seeing the vivid image. Which help them to memorize more information. If you’re the visual learner, studying with pictures, highlights and charts can make you improve in your lessons.

Auditory

In PBL (Project-based-learning) , we mostly use the method of Auditory. The auditory learners Mostly learn by physical activities such as doing, touching, and movements. This method is mostly used for kids. Auditory learning style make the kids more focus, Primary schools mostly teach the students by this method. If you’re an auditory learner, I recommend you to try PBL. Which can improve your learning skills.

Kinesthetic

Kinesthetic learning method is learnt by thinking during the learning progress. If you love to think a lot in your work progress, you might probably a kinesthetic learner. Listening to music, chewing the gum and studying in the quite-space will help you a lot in journey. The kinesthetic learner can be mostly keen on writing, which can make their imagination and thinking wider.

Condensing and Summarizing

Condensing and Summarizing methods involve reducing large amount of information down into something shorter for example notes, images or diagrams.

Key words:

help with memory recall by condensing large amounts of information into a single word or short phrase that is easy to remember. Noting down or highlighting the key words are way to use the keyword method.

Mind maps:

Mind-maps help to generate ideas and make associations. They are a powerful memory aid in an examination because they are visual in nature and show the relationships between component topics within an overall subject area.

Concept maps:

This is a way of representing relationships between ideas images or words in the same way that a road map represents the locations of highways and towns or a circuit diagram would show the working of an electrical appliance.
